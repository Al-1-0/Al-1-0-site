Allan McCafferty is a developer and electronic music producer from Scotland who studied at the University of Glasgow (MA in Digital Media / Information Studies) and University of West of Scotland (MSc Mobile Development).
Allan has also: - contributed music for video art installations, podcasts and radio,
- released music on various labels,
- developed custom web applications for charities and non-profits in the digital giving sector,
- developed archives for arts organisations,
- published digital e-learning materials,
- played electric guitar in a rock band,
and is a father of one. LinkedIn
