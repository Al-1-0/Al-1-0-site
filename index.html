INFO


Allan McCafferty is a web developer, digital archivist, and musician from the dark, west end of Glasgow, Scotland and has an MA in digital media and information studies and a MSc in mobile web development.
A fully-active dev since 2020 and currently working for a digital giving software company called Funraisin, Allan has worked on countless projects for charities/non profits including Irish Cancer Society, Alzheimer's Research UK, RNLI, The Charity for Civil Servants, Dogs Trust, and Mental Health UK.
With Glasgow being the creative city it is, Allan has also contributed music for video art installations, podcasts and radio, developed archives for arts organisations, published digital e-learning materials, and played electric guitar in a rock band.
