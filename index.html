About Me

I am an independent front-end web developer based in the south side of Glasgow, Scotland who has decided to go solo after 4 years experience in making websites. I am trained in web development and also armed with a degree in Digital Media and certificates in User Interface design, responsive web design, JavaScript, and Wordpress!

I spent most of my time during the Covid pandemic in 2020/2021 developing new skills via FreeCodeCamp, and attending a virtual Project Management course. I have also had the pleasure of working freelance for some great people too!

I am comfortable and experienced with the following languages, libraries, and content management systems:


HTML5

CSS3

JavaScript

PHP

I have worked with small-to-medium projects ranging from motion capture studio startups through to art organisations and art curators.

I treat digital content as valuable data, and can ensure that it will be optimised and looking great before deployment.

I am also becoming more and more academically qualified with a MA degree in digital media and info studies at University of Glasgow, a post grad certification in Project Management, and I am currently in my second semester of a MSc in mobile web development at UWS.
